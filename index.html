Who are we?

At our company, we specialise in providing a comprehensive range of high-quality cleaning equipment and supplies to businesses of all types throughout the Southwest. Our product offerings include a wide selection of cleaning chemicals, paper products, and essential cleaning materials such as mops, sponges, buckets, and catering supplies—essentially everything you need to maintain cleanliness and hygiene. We proudly serve a diverse clientele, including schools, pubs, restaurants, care homes, garages, and the food industry, ensuring that each customer receives the specific products they need. Based in Andil House in Trowbridge, we are dedicated to delivering our products directly to your location, making it convenient and efficient for you to maintain a clean and professional environment.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services to our customers to ensure they have the knowledge and resources needed for effective and safe use of our products. Our commitment to customer support and education is reflected in the services we provide.These services are designed to enhance operational efficiency, ensure compliance with safety regulations, and promote best practices in cleaning and hygiene.

Our Story

Clean and Supply Southwest was established in 2023 by Ian Clifford. For several joyful months, Ian and his daughter, Kaylie, operated the business together, building a foundation based on trust, quality, and family values.

In early 2024, our family faced a heart-wrenching challenge when Ian received a terminal cancer diagnosis and passed away a few months later. Despite the immense loss, Ian's wish was for his daughter, Kaylie, to lead the business and uphold his legacy.

Today, Kaylie dedicates herself to honouring her father's memory with unwavering hard work and dedication. She strives to ensure that Clean and Supply Southwest continues to embody the principles and values Ian cherished, serving our community with the same passion and commitment.

Our Suppliers

We collaborate closely with our suppliers to deliver high-quality cleaning products, paper, and materials. They provide us with extensive support, offering in-depth knowledge on product manufacturing, detailed product information, and guidance on adhering to health and safety regulations. This close partnership ensures we can maintain the highest standards of quality, quickly address any issues or special requirements, and continuously enhance customer satisfaction. By leveraging our suppliers' expertise, we can stay ahead of industry trends and deliver innovative solutions to our clients.

Christeyns

Christeyns Professional Hygiene is a prominent provider of innovative hygiene solutions, specialising in the cleaning and disinfection of various environments. Founded in 1946 and headquartered in Belgium, the company has established itself as a global leader in the hygiene industry. We work closely with Christeyns to provide fully accredited cleaning chemicals designed to meet the highest standards of cleanliness and safety in sectors such as healthcare, food processing, hospitality, and industrial applications.

Lucart

Lucart, established in 1953, is a leading European producer of sustainable paper and hygiene products. Renowned for its commitment to environmental sustainability, Lucart transforms recycled materials into high-quality products like toilet paper, kitchen rolls, and hand towels. The company's innovative practices, such as the EcoNatural line, reduce waste and promote recycling. With production facilities across Europe, Lucart maintains high standards of quality and environmental responsibility, evidenced by numerous certifications. Their dedication to customer satisfaction and corporate social responsibility underscores their position as a trusted and forward-thinking supplier in the paper industry.

Robert Scott

Robert Scott, a family-owned business established in 1925, is a leading UK-based manufacturer and supplier of innovative cleaning products and equipment. Specialising in high-quality mops, cloths, brushes, and cleaning solutions, Robert Scott serves a diverse range of sectors, including healthcare, hospitality, and commercial cleaning. The company is committed to sustainability, utilizing recycled materials and eco-friendly processes in their product development. With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, Robert Scott continually evolves its product offerings to meet the changing needs of the cleaning industry, maintaining a strong reputation for reliability and excellence.

Additionally, we supply products from renowned brands like Paperline, Poppies, Cleenol, Star Tissue, and others, ensuring a diverse and reliable range of options for our customers.
